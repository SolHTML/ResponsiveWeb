Struggling?

We get it — some subjects just don’t click, no matter how many times you reread the material or sit through class. Whether you’re stuck on math problems, lost in science, or just trying to keep up with everything at once, it can feel overwhelming.

That’s where we come in. Sol Tutors gives you one-on-one support that actually makes sense. No confusing explanations, no pressure — just real help from someone who knows how to break things down and help you get it. We're here to make school easier, not harder.

Meet Your Tutor!

Hey! I'm Sol — a passionate and patient tutor who's here to help students actually understand what they’re learning, not just memorize it for the test. Through Sol Tutors, I work to make learning clear, simple, and even a little fun.

As a student myself, I know exactly how confusing school can get. That’s why I’ve spent the last few years helping classmates and younger students tackle tough subjects in a way that finally clicks.

I offer tutoring in math, science, English, and more — whether you're in elementary school or middle school, I’ve got you.

No boring lectures. I explain things in a simple, friendly way and adapt to how you learn best. I believe everyone learns differently — and that's totally okay.
